# Business Intelligence Dashboard for Food Delivery Sales Analysis: A Simulated iFood Case

## Overview
This project presents the development of an interactive Power BI dashboard for analyzing food delivery data, based on a simulated dataset inspired by iFood operations. 
The workflow covers data generation, cleaning, modeling, and visualization, enabling insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product and category distribution, and geographic coverage. 
The dashboard is designed to support data-driven decision-making through clear KPIs and interactive visualizations.

## Achievements / Results
- Built an end-to-end Power BI dashboard covering data preparation, modeling, and visualization.
- Delivered clear KPIs for sales performance, customers, products, categories, and geographic reach.
- Enabled interactive analysis by city, product category, and time period.
- Identified sales concentration patterns and high-performing products and regions.
- Demonstrated the practical application of Business Intelligence to support managerial and strategic decision-making.
